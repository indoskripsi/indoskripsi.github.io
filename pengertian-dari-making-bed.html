3. contoh teks prosedur kompleks cara making bed


1. Tarik bed terlebih dahulu untuk memudahkan pengerjaannya
2. Lepaskan linen-linen kotor (striping bed) dengan hati-hati dan periksalah siapa tahu ada barang tamu yang terselip diantara linen tersebut.
3. Periksalah bila ada linen-linen yang rusak
4. Angkat linen kotor masukan ke dalam kantong linen kotor, ganti dengan yang bersih. Pastikan sheet yang akan dipasang tidak bolong dan bernoda (spot)
5. Tebarkan first sheet kemudian masukan sudut paling atas di bagian kepala
6. Tebarkan second sheet dengan posisi jahitan menghadap ke atas dan tepian sheet
bagian atas tepat dipinggir matras
7. Tebarkan blanket dengan ujung tepat di atas second sheet atau tepian atas,turun
kurang lebih 40 cm atau sebesar pillow
8. Tebarkan third sheet sehingga menutup blanket kemudian lipat bersama dengan
second sheet dan blanket sebanyak 2 lipatan kemudian masukan ke dalam bed dan kencangkan serta rapikan
9. Bagian bawah ujung kiri dan kanan dibuat sudut 45° atau 90°
10. Tebarkan bed cover dan pada bagian ujung kepala lipat selebar pillow
11. Pasang pillow dan letakan pillow di bagian atas dengan pillow berada di tengah
hingga kelihata rapih
12. Tutup pillow dengan cover dan rapihkan
13. Dorong dan kembalikan bed ke posisi semula

6. Pengertian tentang law making treaties


Perjanjian yang membentuk hukum (law making treaties), yaitu suatu perjanjian yang melakukan ketentuan-ketentuan hukum bagi masyarakat internasional secara keseluruhan (bersifat multilateral). Perjanjian ini bersifat terbuka bagi pihak ketiga. Contohnya konvensi Wina Tahun 1958 tentang hubungan diplomatik, Konvensi Montiego Tahun 1982 tentang Hukum Laut Internasional, dan sebagainya.yaitu perjanjian yang membentuk hukum bagi masyarakat internasional, perjanjian ini mengikat kepada pihak yang saling mengadakan perjanjian, namun bisa bersift terbuka/flexible bagi pihak ketiga atau pihak lain yang ingin bergabung dalam perjanjian tersebut

21. We would have … the grow bed to plant some kale since the last season if we had … the seeds.A. make-haveB. making-havingC. made-havingD. making-hadE. made-had


Jawaban:

The correct answer is E. made-had.

Penjelasan:

The third conditional is used to express an unreal or hypothetical situation in the past and its possible consequence. It has the following structure:

if + past perfect, ...would + have + past participle

For example:

If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam.

The past perfect tense is formed by using had + past participle. The past participle of make is made and the past participle of have is had. Therefore, the sentence should be:

We would have made the grow bed to plant some kale since the last season if we had had the seeds.

26. contoh dialog making request, making sugestion making complaints, giving instructions


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: Bhs Inggris
Kategori: expression
Kata kunci: request, suggestion, complaint, instruction

1. Making request
J: Sweetie, can you fetch me those chopped onions?
S: Okay, mom. Anyway, would you mind if I go out of town with Carl this weekend?
J: Well, I don't think it's a good idea if only two of you, bring your friend too.
S: But, mom......
J: Sorry, young lady, you're not allowed, unless you're with someone else too.

2.Suggestion
C: Dude, what shall we do this weekend?
D: Shall we play badminton?
C: Not again, man, I'd rather do something else.
D: Oh, okay. Hey, I just remember that there's a big match this weekend, Arsenal vs Liverpool, why don't we watch it live?
C: At the stadium? That'll be awesome.
D: OK, let's get the ticket then.

3.Complaints
O: Hello, how may I help you, Ma'am?
H: Yes, Miss, you can actually. I'd like to file a complaint about one of your porters here. He's been making me uncomfortable during my stay. His name is Patrice. He has been improperly asking me far too many personal questions.
O: Oh, Patrice Lacombe. I shall have him warned then. My apology for that, Ma'am.

4.Instruction
P: Excuse me, could you help me out, please?
W: Yes, how can I help?
P: I'd like to download videos from youtube via Firefox browser. How can you do that?
W: OK, I'll tell you what to so. Follow my lead, okay?
P: Sure.
W: So, first, click the icon on the top right under the 'close' button. Click the 'add-on' and type 'youtube downloader' on search. You'll have many options for that. Just choose one and install it.
P: OK, got it, is that all?
W: Almost, just close the Firefox and open it again. It'll work.
P: Thank you then.
