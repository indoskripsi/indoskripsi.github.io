30. 1) Sinta adds some spoons of sugar into a cup of coffe 2) the man cuts a wood at the back yard 3) the men stuck the ad paper on the wall 4) the plumber repaired the pipes last week


Jawaban:

1. Sinta adds some spoons of sugar into a cup of coffee.

This sentence describes an action that is happening in the present moment. It is in the present simple tense.

2. The man cuts a wood at the back yard.

This sentence also describes an action that is happening in the present moment. However, it is in the active voice. In the active voice, the subject of the sentence (the man) performs the action (cuts a wood).

3. The men stuck the ad paper on the wall.

This sentence describes an action that happened in the past. It is in the simple past tense.

4. The plumber repaired the pipes last week.

This sentence also describes an action that happened in the past. However, it is more specific than the third sentence, because it tells us when the action happened (last week).

Overall, all four sentences are grammatically correct. However, the second sentence is in the active voice, while the other three sentences are in the passive voice. The passive voice is often used in academic writing, but it can also be used in other types of writing to emphasize the object of the sentence.
